About Us


Welcome To Our Bakery


Aesthetic Bakery specializes in the manufacture, supply & distribution and retail of baking ingredients, premixes, accessories and tools. We are passionate about what we do, we produce our own baking products under the brand Aesthetic bakery and in-house brands such as Butteria, Butterich, Cottura, Good Earth and Mama Lena in accordance with the HACCP Based Food Safety System and MeSTI and over 300 of our products are HALAL certified by JAKIM.

At the same time we continuously searching for new and innovative products around the world to bring the best in quality at affordable prices to cater to home baking enthusiasts, professional chefs and food businesses.
